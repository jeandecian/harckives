Welcome to Harckives

Harckives is a cybersecurity knowledge vault, a systematically organized collection of notes, frameworks, practical techniques, and hands-on walkthroughs in the field of information security.

The Purpose: Why Harckives Exists

Harckives serves two core purposes:

  1. My Knowledge Repository: A comprehensive, continually updated resource for defining, documenting, and referencing complex security concepts.

  2. Community Resource: A shared, structured, and high-quality resource for students, practitioners, and enthusiasts in the cybersecurity community.

The Structure: What You’ll Find Here

1. Core Knowledge & Theory

Core theoretical knowledge is organized using the internationally recognized (ISC)² CISSP Common Body of Knowledge (CBK) domains. This ensures comprehensive coverage, structured guides, explanations, and real-world examples, while maintaining professional relevance:

Domain Focus
Domain 1 Security and Risk Management
Domain 2 Asset Security
Domain 3 Security Architecture and Engineering
Domain 4 Communication and Network Security
Domain 5 Identity and Access Management (IAM)
Domain 6 Security Assessment and Testing
Domain 7 Security Operations
Domain 8 Software Development Security

2. Practical Labs

Hands-on walkthroughs and exercises. Each lab includes step-by-step instructions, lessons learned, and tips for further exploration.

3. Cheatsheets

Quick-reference guides for key cybersecurity topics and commands.
